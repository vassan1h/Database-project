About Our Database

Welcome to the Integrated Genome-Scale Metabolic Database!

This platform enables researchers and students within the Segrè Lab to upload, search, view, and download curated genome-scale metabolic models and associated experimental data (like growth condition TSVs).

It features a unified web interface for accessing models based on search criteria or browsing recent uploads. The goal is to facilitate scientific exploration into microbial metabolism by providing a central, accessible repository for models and data generated using various tools and techniques (RASTtk, Model SEED, etc.).

This project was developed as part of the BF768 course under the supervision of Dr. Ilija Dukovski at Boston University.

Developed by Benjamin Pfeiffer, Nicholas White, Nicolas Petrunich, Aravind Panicker, and Vassanth Mathan under the supervision of Dr. Ilija Dukovski. Internal use only. Contact: dukovski@bu.edu
